## Background

**BuildVR by GretXP** is a web and 3D asset creation platform that allows users to build websites strip-by-strip without writing code. The platform supports:

- Website essentials like navigation bars, text sections, buttons, and links  
- Image and video integrations  
- Immersive 3D experiences powered by **Three.js**  
- A seamless, engagement-focused interface  

The internship was designed to bridge **academic learning and industry practices**, emphasizing real-world problem-solving, system thinking, and collaboration.

## Insights into Modern Software Development
